To generate an update of roku channel 
1) update major and build version
2) load a channel on roku device 
3) go to browser and open your roku ip
4) go to utilities and upload last pkg file and enter password and press rekey
5) go to pakager and write channel name and the same password and click on pakage and you got upper a pkg file download link click and download

To generate an build of roku channel
1) load a channel on roku device 
2) go to browser and open your roku ip
3) go to pakager and write channel name and the password and click on pakage and you got upper a pkg file download link click and download
